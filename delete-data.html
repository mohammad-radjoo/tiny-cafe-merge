Tiny Café Merge — Radjoo Games

📋 What Data We Store

Tiny Café Merge stores the following data:

  • Device ID A unique identifier from your device
  • Nickname Your chosen display name (if set)
  • Game Progress Coins, gems, level, board state
  • Leaderboard Your rank and score (if cloud save enabled)

📱 Delete Data From Within the App

The easiest way to delete your data:


1

Open Tiny Café Merge

Launch the app on your device

2

Go to Settings

Tap the ⚙️ Settings icon in the top right corner

3

Find Privacy Section

Scroll to the 🔒 Privacy section

4

Tap "Delete My Data"

Confirm deletion — your cloud data will be permanently removed within 24 hours

📧 Request Deletion by Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request data deletion by emailing us. Please include:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request — Tiny Café Merge"
  • Your device type (Android/iOS)
  • Your nickname (if you set one)
  • Approximate date you last played
⏱️ We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email.
📧 Email Us: privacy@radjoo.com

⚠️ What Happens After Deletion

⚠️ Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your game progress will be lost.

  • All cloud save data will be permanently deleted
  • Your leaderboard entry will be removed
  • Local data on your device will remain (uninstall the app to remove it)
  • Deletion is completed within 30 days of request

🇬🇧 Your UK GDPR Rights

As a UK resident, you have the right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") under UK GDPR. We are committed to honouring your data rights promptly.


For more information, visit our Privacy Policy or contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) .
