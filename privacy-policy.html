Privacy Policy

Tiny Café Merge — Radjoo Games

Last updated: June 2025

📋 Overview

Tiny Café Merge ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Radjoo Games, based in the United Kingdom. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play our game.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do NOT collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary:

  • Device ID: A unique identifier generated from your device, used solely to save your game progress.
  • Nickname: An optional display name you choose for the leaderboard.
  • Game Progress: Coins, gems, level, and board state.
We do NOT collect: real name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • Device ID is used to save and restore your game progress across sessions.
  • Nickname is displayed on the public leaderboard only if you choose to set one.
  • Game data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers to enable cloud save functionality.

☁️ Data Storage

  • Game data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) servers in the United States.
  • Local data is stored on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • We retain your data as long as you use the app. You can request deletion at any time.

🔧 Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Cloud save and leaderboard. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (if applicable): For displaying ads. Privacy Policy

🇬🇧 UK GDPR & Your Rights

As a UK-based developer, we comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR). You have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data.
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@radjoo.com

👶 Children's Privacy

Tiny Café Merge is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

🔒 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase security rules to protect your data. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
